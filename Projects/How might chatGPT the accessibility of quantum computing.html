How might chatGPT the accessibility of quantum computing

The thesis investigates how ChatGPT can improve accessibility to quantum programming for non-experts, that is those who do not fall within the scope of computational quantum mechanics. It focuses on the challenges of "Quantum computers" complexity and the use of ChatGPT to make this technology more accessible and understandable for beginners interested in learning more. The main objective is to demonstrate how a custom GPT model, as the result of Quantum Buddy 2.0, can assist users in generating quantum computing programs, facilitating their understanding and interest in the field.
